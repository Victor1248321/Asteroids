# Asteroid Survivors
Survive against wave after wave of asteroids from all directions for as long as you can. But beware, 
just becuase an asterioid is destroyed, does not mean its pieces cannot still smash you to dust. 

## Motivation
Enjoy a fast paced game reminiscent of the classic asteroids game. 

### Quick Start
Controls to play are simple WASD, using W to move forward, S to move backward, and A and D to turn your ship
